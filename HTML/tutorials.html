Catch the Bait Before it Catch you

BaitCatcher101


Scamming works like fishing. Scammers use baits to trick someone and get something in return from them. As a result of the emergence, the popularity, and the significant use of the internet, scammers also gain a convenient platform for their illegal activities.

BaitCatcher, offers unique ways to raise online scam awareness among players. Through minigames designed for the players to learn by experience, it also provides the players a fun and enjoyable learning environment.



Spot the Red Flag
Select the red flags in the messages. Look for irregularities in the sent messages. Had the message's attitude suddenly changed? Is a message suspicious? That's a red flag that may indicate an online scam.
